
 Zero-Shot Prompting
 --------------------------

The AI is asked to perform a task without examples.

Example: "Write a short paragraph about Artificial Intelligence."

 One-Shot Prompting
 ------------------------

The AI is given one example before performing the task.

Example: Input: Apple → Fruit Input: Carrot → Vegetable Input: Mango → ?

Output: Fruit

Few-Shot Prompting
-----------------------

The AI is given multiple examples.

Example: Dog → Animal Rose → Flower Laptop → Electronic Device Tiger → ?

Output: Animal


Role-Based Prompting
------------------------

The AI is assigned a role.

Example: "Act as a teacher and explain machine learning in simple words."
